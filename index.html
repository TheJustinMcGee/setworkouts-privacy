SetWorkouts

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

SetWorkouts is designed to work without collecting your personal data.

Information Collection

SetWorkouts does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information on servers operated by the developer.

The app does not require account creation and does not use advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking technologies.

On-Device Data

Workout plans, workout history, timer settings, and similar app data are stored locally on your device so the app can function.

If you use the Apple Watch companion app, relevant workout data may sync directly between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple-provided system services.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, SetWorkouts may send local notifications on your device for workout and rest timers. These notifications are created on your device and are not used for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

SetWorkouts does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or external data brokers.

Children's Privacy

Because SetWorkouts does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features or privacy practices change. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, contact the developer using the support information provided on the app's App Store product page.
